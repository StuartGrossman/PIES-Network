About Us

Progress

The inception of PIES was around mid June of 2017. The application was mapped in two weeks, and development began imminently. In less than three months a working MVP had been built, tested, and deployed. This rapid development time shows the dedication of our small team here at PIES.

Team

PIES consists of two core developers and three advisors. Each member of this small team contributes to the vision and high paced development of the network. We encourage you to do your own research on each one of us and build your own opinion of the individuals working hard to bring this vision to life.

Vision

At PIES we believe by offering highly targeted content and respecting users time and data with fair compensation, PIES can reinvent the relationship between users and content publishers of any type.

Principles

Reclaim the value of digital advertising.

Build symbiotic connections between businesses and people.

Stimulate constructive thought and turn creative ideas into reality.
