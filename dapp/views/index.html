PIES Network
Precise Information Exchange System
PIES Network is a blockchain based attention compensation environment focused on secure, honest, and direct transfer of information. In an age where only the large corporations benefit from your personal data, people should have the option of being directly and fully compensated for the attention they give to information - most importantly personally curated advertisement relevant to personal interest. Advertisements shouldn’t be an annoying burden on society requiring extensions to block out, but a meaningful symbiotic relationship focused on minimizing waste and improving efficiency. Pies proposes to be a clean platform where consumers simply get paid for the attention they give to relevant interests.
What is PIES?
Percise

Using your data we will connect you with the best content for you specifically. Our algorithms are designed to make sure that precision is always used when connecting content creators to their potential targets!

Information

Data is the most valuable resource in the world, PIES network wants your data, and wants to pay you what you deserve for it. We are using the best security practices in software to protect and store users data.

Exchange

We see money as information, and we want to distribute and desentrialize the pratices of exchanging it. Our PIES token is a ERC20 Ethereum token, it will serve as the internal currency for our network.

System

We want our users to have a clean, friendly and unobtrusive experience from start to finish. We have built the most up to date web application implementing the best technologies to provide users with the experience they deserve!
