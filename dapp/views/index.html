Our Technology
Q3 2017

At PIES the developement philosophy is to build quick, build secure, go live. This applications development started in late June of 2017 and is nearing completion. At PIES, we believe simplicty is key along with artistic touch and clean responsive design.

PIES stack consists of JavaScript, Ethereum, HTML/CSS, Node, IPFS, Google Cloud and now TenserFlow integration. This stack of technology will create an incredibly reliable application stack that allows for easy scaling and fast development.

PIES beta will launch in late Setember of 2017, less than a month away, long before any other AdTech ICO will have a viable product.

Launch PIES Network
Q3 2017

Once the inital beta is finished the ERC20 token sale will begin. This will be a small token sale only selling around 30% of the network to investors.

After completion of ERC20 token sale the token will be moved on to seval exchanges where it will be publicaly traded. The site will immeditily open up to users to sign up, each user will be evaluated and placed into the network accordingly.

Gathering the user base
Q4 2017

The success of PIES network will rely upon the creation of a valuable, intelligent and healthy group of users.

Value is not just measured in purchasing power, our criteria is going to look at many diverse factors to determine if a candidate is right to join the inital network.

PIES network wants to build a strong reputation among its future clientele and content publishers. By having an incredibly strong group of users, PIES network aims to become as profitable as possible for its clients and users.

Users that join will be highly incentivize to grow the network through their peers and social media. Each time a new user is referred into the network and is accepted, the user who made the reference will receive 10$ worth of our PIES tokens.

Gathering the content publishers
Q4 2017

Once we have a strong inital userbase and a healthy ERC20 token we will start to aquire content publishers based on inital interal data and metrics of users.

Using Tenser flow machine learning data visualizing techniques, PIES network will have an extremely accurate understanding of its user base and will be able to target specific content publisher with high efficiency.

PIES network will offer incentives to new content publishers to allow them to see the power of the network.
